Our patient care journey visualized

Our patient care journey is broken down into 5 simple steps

Patient at home

It is Saturday night. A patient is experiencing unusual symptoms. Eventually, they search their symptoms online. What they find is ambiguous. One of the search results proposes using beyondhealth's symptom checking tool.

Symptom assessment

By adding their symptoms and answering questions, the patient quickly discovers possible causes, as well as their triage level. Results are linked with clinics available in their area.

Appointment booking

From the results screen, the patient easily moves to the clinic’s booking site. The search criteria, based on the symptom check results, are already set, and the user journey is short and efficient. The patient decides to book an appointment

Diagnosis & Clinical Plans

The patient easily shares their test results with healthcare providers through the platform. This enables healthcare professionals to make accurate differential diagnoses and develop tailored clinical plans. The streamlined process improves clinical decisions and reduces wait times.

Emergency Services

In cases of emergencies, our platform ensures swift access to emergency services. With just a few clicks, patients can connect with emergency responders who can provide immediate assistance. This feature is designed to prioritize critical situations and ensure prompt medical attention when every second counts

Continued Care

Our platform facilitates ongoing communication and collaboration between patients and healthcare professionals. Through secure messaging and virtual consultations, patients can easily connect with their healthcare providers, ask questions, and receive guidance on managing their health conditions.
